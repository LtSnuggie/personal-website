The early years

Growing up I was always into sports. I took swim lessons at a young age and basically lived for the pool. My parents couldn't keep me out of the water, and signed me up for a swim team. I continued to swim competitively until I graduated high school.

When I hit middle school I finally found on of my biggest passions, soccer. When I hit High School I was nominated for an international traveling team. The first year we played in Italy and Austria, and the second year we played in Denmark and Sweden. I continued to play club throughout college and now play for a club in Austin called ATXUnited.
